INGREDIENTS FOR MAKING KASHATA

  • 5 Coconuts.
  • 1 tablespoon of crushed cardamon.
  • 1 kg of sugar.
  • 1 teaspoonful of salt.
  • 2 teaspoonful of essence.(vanilla or strawberry depending on the flavour)
  • Food color.
  • mbuzi for grinding coconut.
  • 3 tablespoon of cooking oil.
  • Cooking stick.
  • Alluminium cooking pan.
  • 1 smooth sharp knife.
  • Rolling pin.

INSTRUCTIONS FOR COOKING KASHATA:

~In your cooking pan on medium heat, pour water, vanilla essence, food color and sugar. Do not stir the sugar in the pan as it will form crystals. Holding the pan, swirl the syrup around in the pan until all the sugar is disssolved. Keep cooking the syrup until it thickens. If you put the syrup between your thumb and your index finger, the syrup should be sticky and stringy. At this point you know your sugar is ready for the next step.

~Add the coconut flakes, cardamon and salt to the syrup. Stir evenly as you add each ingredient. When all ingredients have been added to the mixture, continue stirring for at most 10minutes, ensuring everything is well mixed. When the mixture starts to form a ball and pull away from the cookin pan is when it is ready, and can be removed.

~Use your cooking stick to remove the mixture onto a well oiled flat and smooth surface, and spread it evenly using the rollin pin use a sharp knife to cut the kashata into any shape of your choice. You can leave it to dry overnight to achieve the crunchy taste or you can leave for a few hours and serve.

INGREDIENTS FOR MAKING ACHARI

  • Raw Mangoes.(not too ripe)
  • 1/4 kg of sugar.
  • 1 glass of water.
  • Food coloring.
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • Red chilli powder
  • Vegetable oil

INSTRUCTIONS FOR COOKING ACHARI

~Wash the mangoes, peel them and cut into pieces(thin slices).Place the sliced mangoes in a container and add enough salt to taste, and let it sit overnight for two days. After two days drain the slices of mangoes and discard the water.

~Then spread the sliced mangoes and dry them out in the sun(this might take longer to dry depending on the amount of sun received in an area).

Once the mangoes have dried put them in a container and add remaining ingredients according to your taste.

~Pour water in a cooking pan add sugar, salt and food color. Heat the mixture up until it thickens and add the dried mangoes and ensure the heat is low, stir evenly. Remove and let it cool for about 8-12 minutes now it is ready.

INGREDIENTS FOR MABUYU

  • 2 cups of sugar.
  • 2 cups of water.
  • 1/2 cup mabuyu flour.
  • 4 cups of mabuyu.
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
  • Food color(red or preffered color)(red or preffered color).
  • Essence(vanilla or strawberry).
  • 1/4teaspoon ground cardamon.
  • 1/4 teaspoon of chilli(optional).

INSTRUCTIONS FOR COOKING MABUYU

~In a pot add water.

~Add sugar (chilli powder) chilli powder is optional.

~Mix all the ingredients thorougly and let it come to a boil.

~Add food color, essence and ground cardamon and cook on medium heat.

~Bubble and foam will be seen in the cooking pot, continue stirring and let it continue to cook.

~The syrup will start to bubble down, hence it will start thickening now add the mabuyu and mix well.

~Continue to mix the mabuyu seeds in the cooking pot and then add the mabuyu flour and mix well, continue to cook until the syrup starts to crystalize and it coats the mabuyu seeds.

~Remove the sugar coated mabuyu and place in a wide plate and let it cool completely. The mabuyu is ready to be eaten.

~You can pack as shown.
